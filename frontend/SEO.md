## Files Added

- `public/robots.txt` tells crawlers what to index and points to the sitemap.
- `public/sitemap.xml` lists public routes.
- `public/manifest.webmanifest` improves mobile installability and brand metadata.
- `public/_redirects` keeps SPA routes crawlable on Netlify.
- `public/llms.txt` gives AI crawlers a compact project summary.
- `public/og-image.svg` gives social previews a consistent 1200x630 image.
